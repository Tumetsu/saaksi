Looking for Finnish weather data in spreadsheet compatible format?

With Saaksi you are able to download open weather data of Finnish Meteorological Institute without programming knowledge. Choose a topic of interest below to begin.


Daily weather

Access FMI's daily weather observations from stations in Finland.

Sea level

Access FMI's daily sea level observations from stations in Finland.

Air quality

Access FMI's air quality observations from stations in Finland.

About

Saaksi is a free open-source tool for downloading Finnish Meteorological data.

Call To Action!

Saaksi is continuum to earlier desktop tool for downloading Finnish weather data for research purposes. FMI provides open weather data through programmable API but not a service for non-programmers to easily access the data. Saaksi strives to fill this need.
